Welcome to my BLOG!

This is a story of a once upon a time curious, confused, lost, rebellious child. Early in life, I had so many questions. I still have so many questions, only difference is I barely ask them anymore. Life has its way of shaping you, do I like my shape? Physically-yes, mentally-not so much.

I’ve lost a lot of my curiosity, is this what trauma does to you? trauma from poverty? I like to think of poverty's methods as silent chisels that carve deep lines into the face of hope. I probably lost a lot of enthusiasm due to having a really poor mentality, lack of exposure also played a big part. Now that I think of it, what more exposure could I have asked for? I had my tertiary education at the prestigious Obafemi Awolowo University and I still didn’t get enough exposure? that’s funny and wrong! I got exposed to the wrong things the wrong way of thinking. I guess that counts as no exposure anyways.

But, enough of that. There's something exposure does something to you, I can say with a lot of confidence that I’ve learnt a lot in the past few months due to a change of mentality and proper exposure, I mean, I’m here learning how to code and staying consistent with it(I can’t remember the last time I was consistent with anything). Poverty was a barrier early in life, or so I thought, however with a change in mentality and more exposure I’ve come to realize poverty is the whip that drives desperation. Applying to AltSchool Africa was out of desperation; desperation to make a name for family, desperation to add value to myself, desperation to change my standard of living.

I’m happy I took that huge step, cause it kickstarted a chain reaction, I saw the power of daily improvement from consistently writing code and it has impacted several aspects of my life. It has taught me that I can become a better person by putting in 1% daily towards self-improvement. Enough said, need to go write some code. I’m starting to enjoy writing btw, it’s so calming. Ps; I wrote this hoping no one would read this, do not mind my writing, I’m new to this.
